Aloo Paratha Recipe

Delicious and stuffed flatbread with spiced mashed potatoes, a perfect breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 ½ cups boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed to knead the dough
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

You can watch this video for reference

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and a pinch of salt. Gradually add water and knead the mixture into a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, combine the boiled potatoes, chopped onions (if using), green chilies, ginger, cumin seeds, garam masala, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix everything well to make the stuffing.
  3. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls. Flatten one dough ball into a small disc using your fingers or a rolling pin.
  4. Place a spoonful of the potato mixture in the center of the flattened dough disc and bring the edges together to seal the filling inside. Pinch the edges together to form a ball again.
  5. Now, gently roll out the stuffed dough ball into a flat circle, ensuring the stuffing doesn’t spill out.
  6. Heat a tawa or griddle on medium heat. Once hot, place the rolled paratha onto the tawa and cook for about 1-2 minutes until bubbles start to form on the surface.
  7. Flip the paratha and cook the other side. Apply ghee or oil to both sides and cook until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove from the tawa and serve hot with yogurt, pickles, or raita.

Enjoy your delicious Aloo Paratha!
